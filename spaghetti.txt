
Ingredients

18 oz spaghetti
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound ground beef
1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
Salt and pepper, to taste
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
Add mor cheese

Instructions

Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente, about 10 minutes. Drain and return to the pot.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat olive oil and cook onion and garlic, stirring occasionally, until soft, about 5 minutes. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up the meat with a wooden spoon, until browned and cooked through, about 15 minutes. Drain the excess fat.
Stir in marinara sauce, salt and pepper and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened, about 10 minutes.
Stir in basil and taste for seasoning. Adjust as needed.
Serve the spaghetti topped with the meat sauce and sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
